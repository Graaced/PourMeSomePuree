# PourMeSomePuree
In this minigame, you will have to survive hordes of sword-wielding skeletons;

# HOW TO PLAY
Use WASD to move;
Spacebar to attack;
E key to open chests;

# OBJECTS IN GAME
Break the ampoules to gain life;
Accumulate coins from skeleton kills;
Open the chests when you have accumulated enough coins to get power ups;

# POWER-UP
Damage (SWORD ICON);
Stamina (AMPOULE ICON);
Invulnerability (SHIELD ICON)
